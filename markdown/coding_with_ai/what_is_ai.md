## what is AI
* machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. 
* popular types: 
  * computer vision: facial regognition, object detection, and image classification.
  * predictive AI: recommender systems, fraud detection, and predictive maintenance.
  * generative AI: text generation, image synthesis, and music composition.
  * natural language processing (NLP): sentiment analysis, language translation, and chatbots.
  
## llm:
* large language models (LLMs) are a type of AI that can understand and generate human-like text.
